3D Model Cube

A cube is a three-dimensional geometric solid with six square faces, twelve edges, and eight vertices. All edges of a cube are of equal length, and all faces are squares. Dice, sugar cubes, and Rubik's Cubes are examples of objects that are shaped like cubes.

3D Necker Cube

The "metal cube illusion" likely refers to either the Necker cube, a famous visual illusion, or a physical metal cube designed to create a similar illusion of movement or ambiguity. The Necker cube is a wireframe cube that spontaneously switches perspective, appearing to switch between two distinct orientations, illustrating the brain's ambiguous interpretation of visual data. Metal cubes, like the "MetMo Cube", can be crafted to evoke a similar sense of visual ambiguity or movement, playing with how the viewer perceives depth and shape.
